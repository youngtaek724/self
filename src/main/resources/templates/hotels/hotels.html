골든튤립 제주성산호텔은 제주 국제공항에서 차로 약 1시간 10분이 소요됩니다. 주변 관광지인 광치기해변은 도보로 약 15분 거리에 있으며, 세계 관광명소인 성산 일출봉은 차로 약 5분, 신양섭지해수욕장은 차로 약 8분이면 갈 수 있습니다. 호텔에는 루프탑 수영장, 피트니스센터의 즐길 거리가 있으며, 편의점, 비즈니스센터도 마련되어 있습니다. 조식을 제공하는 뷔페 레스토랑과 중식당도 갖춰져 있습니다.

    "There must be better hotels around"

    A lot of research went into our stay at Seongsan, I hesitated to book the Golden Tulip but it appeared the best of the hotel in the region for our needs. Check-in was smooth, on entering the room for the first time our room was pleasant and of an adequate size. The bathroom was dimly lit, neat and clean. Our issues were that the hotel had disabled the air-conditioning with the only option of leaving the balcony sliding door open for ventilation and to cool the room down. Only problem with that is the traffic noise which finally settled late at night but begins again before dawn. The bed was made with a fitted sheet and a quilt, no top sheet. The mattress was satisfactory but the pillows were terribly soft becoming flat once used. The hotel shows signs of age. Breakfast was not included in our room but I purchased breakfast coupons from reception before 11 pm at a slightly discounted rate; children under 3 years eat free. The breakfast buffet was extensive with the majority being Korean fare, there was an adequate amount of western choices all being much sweeter than expected. There even was ice-cream and waffles. Minimal/no fresh fruit, mostly tinned or frozen (lychees/rambutans). Parking was on-site and very narrow. There were plenty of restaurants in walking distance of the hotel and a fabulous bakery. The biggest disappointment was housekeeping, while the towels were changed daily and bottled water supplied, the making of the beds was substandard, a standard I have never come across and had hoped not to encounter again until our stay in Seogwipo!!! It looked like no attempt had been made to make the beds neatly, instead the quilt was 'thrown' over the bed, over the top of the displaced pillows, it looked an absolute mess! I needed to correct straight away for our my pleasing.Had I have known that the air-conditioning would be disabled leaving the room too warm at night for sleeping comfortably, needing to have the balcony door open to cool the room down but then encounter traffic noise and that housekeeping were unable to make a bed, I would not have stayed at the Golden Tulip Seongsan.

    "Value for money hotel with huge rooms"

    We stumble on this hotel by chance as we were looking for a hotel with larger rooms. Rooms in most hotels in the Seongsan area are less than 30 m2. Golden Tulip Seongsan offers Premium Suites which has a separate bedroom with king size bed and a double bed in a separate room. Bathroom also has a separate shower and jacuzzi. You get beautiful views of Seongsan Ilchubong from the room. Breakfast at the hotel is a bit pricey at 15,000 won. There are 2 bakeries just outside the hotel and a large CU where you can grab food. It is also about 8 min walk to Goseong Five Day Market which is well worth a visit. Note that the hotel has very limited onsite parking so if you arrive late in the evening, you will be directed to park at an open air free parking area across the hotel.
